Dice Advice
for
Warhammer Underworlds

Calculate the probabilities of combat outcomes in Warhammer Underworlds. ▼

Input the attacker and defender's weapon and defensive stats in the appropriate inputs. The success target is a conversion of the Underworlds combat dice to a traditional d6; lower the number by one for flanking and by two for surrounding.

This web app uses a Monte Carlo simulation rather than a direct probabilistic calculation; it simulates a large number of combats and tallies the results. This is easier to implement but gives inexact results, with a higher number of simulations leading to more accuracy. You can specify the number of combats to simulate.
